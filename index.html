How product matching works

Every product listing from Pet Valu, PetSmart, Chewy, and Amazon.ca is compared using four match strictness levels, ordered from strictest to broadest.

L0 requires an identical barcode. L1 matches the same brand, product line, and size (flavor may differ). L2 relaxes to comparable size buckets within the same line. L3 compares at the brand-and-format level when product lines differ.

Strictest — identical product
Broadest — same-brand comparable

How strictness works

Levels are nested, not independent. Selecting a broader strictness automatically includes every stricter match. Choosing L2 counts SKUs that match at L0, L1, or L2 — you cannot mix arbitrary combinations.

  • Each SKU pair has one strictest (best) match level in the backend.
  • The dashboard shows all pairs whose best level is at or below your selected maximum.
  • Stricter = higher confidence the products are the same item; broader = more assortment overlap.
